// HOW QUANTUM MECHANICS WORKS IN THIS GAME
🌊 Superposition

Each ship spans 2 cells at 50% each. A hidden quantum coin-flip decides the real location. Both cells exist simultaneously until measured.

📏 Measurement Collapse

Firing = quantum measurement. Hit the real cell → ship destroyed. Miss → wavefunction collapses and the ship fully appears in the other cell (100% certain).

🔗 Entanglement

Both cells of a ship are entangled. Measuring one instantly determines the other — exactly like real quantum entanglement.

🎯 Strategy

A miss reveals the ship's exact position. Fire at the 100% cell on your next turn for a guaranteed hit. Missing is information, not failure.
